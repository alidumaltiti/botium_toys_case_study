🛡️ Botium Toys: Security Audit & Compliance Assessment

📌 Project Overview

This repository hosts the interactive portfolio case study and comprehensive audit report for the Botium Toys Security Assessment. This simulated enterprise audit was completed as a major milestone in Module 2 of the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate program, facilitated by INCO Academy and Coursera.

The objective of this project was to act as a Cybersecurity Auditor to evaluate the administrative, technical, and physical controls of an organization operating with significant security blind spots, and to build a roadmap to resilience.

🚨 The Challenge (Baseline Assessment)

Botium Toys lacked foundational security governance, exposing the business to severe regulatory fines and a high likelihood of a data breach.

Initial Risk Score: 8/10 (High Risk)

Critical Vulnerabilities:

Cardholder data stored in plaintext.

Complete lack of disaster recovery or backup strategies.

Absent access governance (No RBAC or MFA).

Compliance Gaps: Significant shortfalls identified across PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC 2 standards.

💡 The Solution & Strategic Impact

Using the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF), I mapped out the organization's risk profile and engineered a strategic, 6-month phased remediation roadmap.

Key Outcomes:

Designed prioritized remediation strategies targeting high-impact vulnerabilities.

Projected a >60% reduction in overall breach likelihood.

Established a clear pathway to regulatory compliance, transforming the environment from a liability into a resilient infrastructure.

🛠️ Skills & Frameworks Demonstrated

Frameworks & Regulations: NIST CSF, PCI DSS, GDPR, SOC 2

Core Competencies: Risk Assessment, Compliance Gap Analysis, Security Auditing, Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC), Business Continuity Planning.

Tools/Methodologies: Risk Heat Mapping, Controls Evaluation (Administrative, Technical, Physical).
